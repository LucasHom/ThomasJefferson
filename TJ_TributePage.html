Here is a Timeline of Thomas Jefferson's life:


  • 1743 - Thomas Jefferson was Born at Shadwell on April 13, 1743

  • 1760-62 - Thomas Jefferson attended the College of William and Mary

  • 1768 - Elected to House of Burgesses. Began the leveling of the Monticello mountantop

  • 1826 - Jefferson died at Monticello on July 4th. John Adams also died on this same day at his home in Quincy, Massachusetts.
